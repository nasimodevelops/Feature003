Behind the Band

"We have known each other since elementary school, and started making music together in 2019. Since then we played with the idea of being in a band and started writing songs for fun. The very first idea for a band we had was a genreless comedy group inspired by bands like Ninja Sex Party and Flight of the Conchords. In a fit of tired inspiration one summer night, Colton wrote the framework for the song “Streetlights.” After getting it stuck in our heads several times, we decided it would be a good first song to record. Soon after, we decided on the name irl and continued to write songs under this name."

About irl

Musical Infulences

We will have an EP out by the end of this year that will be available on Spotify, Amazon, Apple, Pandora, and Youtube. Our inspirations are far-reaching and wide, and we think it comes out in our music. We both grew up on classic rock and still occasionally listen to the Beatles, Pink Floyd, and Toto. Other influences include Her’s, Mitski, Penelope Scott, Bowling Shoes, Cavetown, Snail Mail, Beach Bunny, and Sidney Gish.

Genre of Music

Our genre is sort of whatever kind of music we feel like making at the time, but generally say it is alternative pop/indie pop if we are made to pick one. We have songs ranging from dream pop to jazz to acoustic in addition to a more traditional indie pop sound. Henry and Colton both play all the instruments in the band and both sing.
